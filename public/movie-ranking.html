The Best Movies According to Terry

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Harry Potter

I enjoyed the entire Harry Potter series. I believe the reason is I was able to share the movie experience with my kids and it's a memory I reflect on every time I see it.

Lord of the Rings

Again I enjoyed the entire series simply because it takes you through the journey with all the characters.

Iron Man

This was the beginning of the Marvel universe. It started the franchise on a variety of adventures with different heroes but, I would say Iron Man is my favorite.
